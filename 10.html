Manifesto on the Future of Humans and Artificial Intelligence

We are living in the era of AI (artificial intelligence), advancing at an exponential rate.

Given the current rate, it is probably less than 10 years before AI will penetrate most domains of human life.

If we continue to make progress without restrictions after that, we will probably end up losing almost everything to AI (ex, people’ jobs, and even creativity, which was considered unique to humans)

In order not to go to such a situation, we must learn from history and consider the coexistence of AI and humans.

As Hegel put it, “History and experience teach us that people and governments have never learned anything from history or drawn out principles and acted upon them.”

Now is the time to get out of the human ignorance discovered by Hegel and learn from history

Also, Karl Popper, the godfather of the science philosophy, said:

“Attempts to build heaven on earth always create hell.”

Perhaps it could be interpreted that we should be somewhat wary of the development of AI?

Eventually, we can see that the development of AI needs to be limited by referring to the historical cases of the 2nd industrial revolution and the words of Karl Popper

I think now it’s the time to positively accomodate Ulrich Beck's concept of 'reflexive modernization' (a concept that promotes that humans should enhance their control over science by considering not only the advantages of modern science and technology, but also its limitations).

It's not too late.

From now on, how about establishing a policy that can promote the coexistence of humans and AI and promising a better future by creating AI that can achieve the coexistence of humans and AI?

Park Chan-hyung, Lee Seung-jun, Choi Eun-seong, and Seo Hyeon-gyu
